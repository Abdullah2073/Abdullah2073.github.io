End-to-end ML pipeline predicting flight delays from merged flight operations and weather data. Deep EDA uncovered key delay drivers including route congestion and weather severity. Final model selected on balanced Accuracy, Precision, Recall, and F1-score, with SHAP explainability validating feature importance.
